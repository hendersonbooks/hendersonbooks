Descendants of Thomas HENDERSON Jr.

Information on the family below begins in Fairfax [now Loudoun] VA in 1755 and follows to Pittlsyvania, VA in the 1760's and 70's. By the late 1770's they are in Wilkes, NC. By 1786 the family is in Laurens, SC. Thomas Henderson d in 1806 in Laurens and Frances d in 1811 in Luarens, SC
